About Member Solutions

In 1991, we formed Member Solutions based on the idea that relationships are at the heart of every business success.

By prioritising people in our billing system, we unlocked a path for martial artists and fitness pros to thrive in their passionwhile achieving financial success. Through the years, withthousands of leaders on board, we swiftly evolved to provide member management software solutions.

Today, we offer a complete member management and delinquency management platform designed to help membership businesses of all shapes and sizes reach peak operational efficiencyand accelerate their cash flow. From the very first member enrollment to the pinnacle of their achievements, were honored to be a part of the journey.
