Billing Services

Increase profits and cash flow with the backing of our dedicated team of billing professionals. Our proven, proprietary billing and member management solutions for martial arts schools and fitness gyms deliver member payments on time. Every time. Like clockwork.

Core Features

  • Inbound, U.S.-based professional call center
  • Interactive Voice Response (IVR) phone payment system
  • EFT and credit card processing
  • Flexible member billing dates
  • Real-time financial reporting
  • Contract administration and management of terms
  • Online member portal
  • Past-due account/delinquency management program
